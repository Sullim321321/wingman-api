Privacy Policy

Wingman Travel  ·  Last updated: June 25, 2026

Overview

Wingman Travel ("we", "our", or "us") is a flight disruption prediction app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information needed to provide the service:

  • Email address — used to send you a one-time login code. We do not store passwords.
  • Push notification token — used to send you flight disruption alerts. Only stored if you grant notification permission.
  • Flight route data — departure and arrival airports you enter are used to fetch live weather data and calculate disruption risk. This data is not linked to your identity.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you via email one-time code
  • To send you push notifications about flight disruption risk (only if you opt in)
  • To provide flight disruption predictions based on live weather data

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except as described below.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Resend — sends login code emails. Your email address is transmitted to Resend for this purpose.
  • Upstash Redis — stores temporary login codes (deleted after 10 minutes).
  • Neon PostgreSQL — stores your email address and push notification token.
  • Expo Push Notifications — delivers push alerts to your device.
  • aviationweather.gov — provides live METAR weather data. No personal data is sent to this service.

Data Retention

Login codes are automatically deleted after 10 minutes. Your email address and push token are retained for as long as you use the app. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Push Notifications

Push notifications are entirely optional. You can disable them at any time in your device's Settings app under Notifications → Wingman Travel. Disabling notifications does not affect your ability to use the app.

Children's Privacy

Wingman Travel is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at: privacy@welcometothefight.club
