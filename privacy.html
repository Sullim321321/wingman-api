Privacy Policy

Wingman Travel  ·  Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Wingman Travel ("we", "our", or "us") is a premium travel disruption and concierge app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide our service:

  • Account Information: Phone number and email address used for authentication.
  • Travel Information: Flight itineraries, hotel bookings, and travel preferences. When you opt-in to email integration, we securely scan for travel-related confirmations.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise location data to provide real-time contextual updates (e.g., airport navigation, local weather).
  • Communications: Messages sent to the Wingman Concierge and preferences regarding notifications.

How We Use Your Information

  • To monitor flights and travel itineraries for delays, cancellations, and gate changes.
  • To provide proactive rebooking options, alternative flights, and ground transport recommendations.
  • To communicate with you via push notifications, SMS, or in-app chat regarding your travel status.
  • To contact hotels or restaurants on your behalf during disruptions, strictly based on your instructions.

We never sell your personal data. Your trust is our core product.

Data Security & Third-Party Services

We implement robust security measures, including token rotation and encryption, to protect your data. We use the following third-party services strictly to operate the app:

  • Twilio: Delivers SMS authentication codes and disruption alerts.
  • PointMe: Provides award flight search data.
  • Cover Genius: Provides travel protection services.
  • AI Providers: Sensitive Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is stripped from concierge messages before processing by third-party AI models.

SMS and Communications (A2P 10DLC)

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your travel itineraries, disruption alerts, and authentication (OTP). Message frequency varies based on your travel activity. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Data Retention & Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can disconnect your email account or disable location services directly within the app. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at: support@wingman.travel
